Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

IronCore Fit AI ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google. We do not access your Google password.

Fitness Data: Workouts, meals, body measurements, progress photos, and other fitness information you enter into the app. This data is stored in your private Firebase account.

Camera Data: When you use the AI Form Coach, your phone camera captures video for real-time pose detection. This processing happens entirely on your device using TensorFlow.js. No video or images from the Form Coach are uploaded to our servers or stored remotely.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics (pages viewed, features used, crash reports) via Firebase Analytics to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalize your fitness experience
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase
  • To power AI coaching features (workout recommendations, form analysis)
  • To display leaderboards, arena battles, and guild features
  • To process payments via Razorpay (we do not store payment card details)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) with security rules that restrict access to your account only. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We use Firebase Authentication to verify your identity.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, storage, analytics
  • Razorpay — Payment processing (Premium subscriptions)
  • Google Gemini API — AI coaching responses (your prompts are sent to Google's API)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your fitness data is never shared with third parties for advertising. Leaderboard data (username, rank, XP) is visible to other app users as part of the competitive features.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app.
  • Delete: You can request account deletion by contacting us. We will delete all your data from our systems within 30 days.
  • Export: You can request a copy of your data by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

IronCore Fit AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at: privacy@ironcore.ai
