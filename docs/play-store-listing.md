## Full Description (4000 chars max)

IronCore AI turns your fitness journey into a competitive sport.

This isn't another workout tracker that you'll forget in a week. IronCore AI is a full competitive fitness platform — with PvP battles, ranked leagues, team guilds, and an AI coach that actually pushes you to improve.

BATTLE YOUR FRIENDS
Challenge anyone to a head-to-head workout battle. Both players log their exercise, and the server scores you on reps, form quality, and intensity. Winner gets XP, Elo rating boost, and bragging rights. Loser gets motivated.

RANKED LEAGUES
Climb from Iron Novice to Diamond. Your league rank updates based on XP earned from workouts, battles, and daily challenges. See where you stand on the global leaderboard.

GUILDS & TEAM WARS
Create or join a guild with your gym crew. Contribute weekly XP to your guild's war score. Top guilds earn exclusive rewards. Stronger together.

AI COACH
Get personalized workout guidance powered by AI. Ask questions about form, nutrition, recovery — the coach adapts to your goals and fitness level. 3 free sessions per day, unlimited with Premium.

COMPLETE WORKOUT TRACKING
Log strength training, cardio, and bodyweight workouts with detailed set/rep/weight tracking. See your volume, streaks, and progress over time.

NUTRITION TRACKING
Log meals with AI-powered macro estimation. Snap a photo of your food and get instant calorie, protein, carb, and fat breakdown.

GAMIFICATION THAT ACTUALLY WORKS
- Forge Streak: Don't break the chain. Daily login streaks with XP multipliers
- Battle Pass: Seasonal challenges with exclusive rewards
- Achievements: 50+ badges to unlock across all categories
- Community Boss: Join the whole community to take down massive raid bosses
- XP & Leveling: Every workout, meal, and battle earns XP toward your next level

PREMIUM FEATURES
- Unlimited AI Coach sessions
- Full league access with ranked battles
- Guild creation and leadership
- Advanced analytics and progress reports
- Priority matchmaking

Free to use with generous limits. Premium unlocks the full competitive experience.

Built by a solo developer who got tired of boring fitness apps. IronCore AI is what happens when you combine the progression systems of competitive gaming with real-world fitness.

Your phone. Your trainer. Your arena.
